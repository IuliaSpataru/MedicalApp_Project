
SynCCare: Synchronized Care
Application Description: SynCCare

The idea for SynCCare originated from a recent hospital experience where a family member was hospitalized
and unable to communicate health-related information due to their critical condition.
In most public hospitals, you cannot simply call the reception desk to inquire about the status of an
admitted patient. This became especially evident during the pandemic when visits were restricted, leaving family
 members without a way to find out what was happening if the hospitalized individual couldn’t communicate via phone.

How the application works:

1. WEBSITE
A user-friendly and simple interface where relatives can access the following:

MENU
DEPARTMENTS
PRICING
RATING
CONTACT
LOGIN/LOGOUT
LANGUAGE SWITCH (RO/EN)

ELECTRONIC MEDICAL FILE
A patient’s relative creates an account and can view the following details in the electronic medical file:

Admission date/discharge date (if applicable).
Daily test results.
Cleaning/disinfection log for the room.
Bed linen change log.
Current treatment/new treatment (with accompanying explanation).
Whether the patient had breakfast/lunch/dinner or refused meals.
General mood and condition.
The timestamp and details of the last update to the file, including who made the update.
Items the patient might need.
Treatment plan prescribed by the doctor, including a progress summary.
Additional observations.
In this section, the relative can also request an appointment (via phone or in-person) with the attending physician,
depending on their availability, by filling out a short questionnaire.


2. INTERNAL MANAGEMENT INTERFACE
An administrative interface for medical staff that allows real-time updates to patient files.
This interface operates based on a predefined user hierarchy with roles and permissions:

The hospital is divided into 6 DEPARTMENTS:

Cardiology
Rheumatology
Pulmonology
Oncology
Gastroenterology
Kinetotherapy


Each department has the following structure ---> User Roles and Permissions <---

***CERTIFIED NURSE - LEVEL 1 USER***
Updates when the room was disinfected or bed linens were changed.
Records whether the patient had breakfast/lunch/dinner or refused the meal.
Can view and mark assigned tasks as completed.
Cannot access other patient details.
Add necessary items for the patient to be brought.


***LICENSED NURSE - LEVEL 2 USER***

Records vital signs and recent test results.
Can view and mark assigned tasks as completed.
Can assign tasks to CERTIFIED NURSE(1).
View all activities of CERTIFIED NURSE(1).



***ATTENDING PHYSICIAN - LEVEL 3 USER***

Can add/modify treatments.
Can view and mark assigned tasks as completed.
Can assign tasks to LICENSED NURSE(2).
Can view activities of CERTIFIED NURSE(1) and LICENSED NURSE(2).



***DOCTOR ON DUTY - LEVEL 4 USER***

Can add/modify treatments.
Can view and mark assigned tasks as completed.
Can assign tasks to LICENSED NURSE(2).
Can view activities of CERTIFIED NURSE(1) and LICENSED NURSE(2).



***HEAD OF DEPARTMENT - LEVEL 5 USER***

Can add/modify treatments.
Can view and mark assigned tasks as completed.
Can assign tasks to ATTENDING PHYSICIAN(3) and DOCTOR ON DUTY(4).
Can view activities of CERTIFIED NURSE(1), LICENSED NURSE(2), ATTENDING PHYSICIAN(3) and DOCTOR ON DUTY(4).
Can download comprehensive patient reports for any given time period for any patient in his ward.
Can view and edit financial reports, work schedules, and employment contracts for their ward.
Can access all active (admitted) and inactive (discharged) patients in their ward, over any time period.



***HOSPITAL MANAGER - LEVEL 6 USER***

Can assign tasks for CERTIFIED NURSE(1), LICENSED NURSE(2), ATTENDING PHYSICIAN(3), DOCTOR ON DUTY(4) and HEAD OF DEPARTMENT(5).
Can view activities for ALL USER LEVELS (1)-(2)-(3)-(4)-(5).
Can view, download, and edit all financial reports, work schedules, and employment contracts across the entire hospital.
Can add/modify treatments and instructions for ALL USER LEVELS (1)-(2)-(3)-(4)-(5).
Can download complete patient reports for any specified time period, for all the wards in the hospital.
Can access, create and edit all reports related to medical insurance data, supplier contracts, budget and equipments.





SynCCare: Grija sincronizata
Descrierea aplicatiei:  *SynCCare*

Ideea a pornit de la o experienta relativ recenta in spital, cand unul din membrii familiei, internat fiind, nu putea
sa comunice informatii despre starea de sanatate, aflandu-se intr-o stare destul de dificila.
La majoritatea spitalelor de stat, nu poti suna la receptie, de exemplu, sa intrebi ce face persoana internata.
Pandemiei este un exemplu extrem de bun, pentru ca vizitele au fost interzise si efectiv, nu
aveai nici o posibilitate de a afla ce se intampla, daca persoana internata era incapabila de a comunica prin telefon.

Aplicatia ar functiona astfel:

1. Partea de WEBSITE - Cu o interfata prietenoasa si simpla, unde:
MENIU:
        - SECTII
        - TARIFE
        - RATING
        - CONTACT
        - LOGIN/LOGOUT
        - LIMBA RO/EN
        - FISA ELECTRONICA

Apartinatorul pacientului isi creeaza un cont si poate vedea urmatoarele in fisa electronica:
- data internarii / data eventualei externari;
- rezultatele analizelor efectuate zilnic;
- cand a fost dezinfectat salonul;
- cand au fost schimbate lenjeriile de pat;
- tratamentul curent/ noul tratament cu motivatia de rigoare;
- daca pacientil a mancat mic dejun/ pranz / cina sau a refuzat una sau toate mesele oferite;
- starea de spirit generala;
- ora, data la care a avut loc ultima modificare in aceasta fisa electronica si de catre cine a fost modificata;
- lucuri de care pacintul ar putea avea nevoie;
- schema de tratament pe care o prevede medicul impreuna cu o concluzie din partea acestia in legatura cu progresul;
- alte observatii;

--> De asemnea, in aceasta sectiune, apartinatorul poate solicita o programare telefonica sau o programare in
unitatea medicala, cu medicul curant, in functie de disponibilitatea  calendarului acestuia, prin intermediul unui
scurt chestionar.


2. Partea de Administrare Interna - O interfata pentru STAFF

In aceasta parte administrativa, se face updatarea fisei in timp real de catre tot personalul medical din unitate,
avand la baza o ierarhie predefinita cu useri si permisiuni in functie de:

Am avea 6 SECTII: CARDIOLOGIE, REUMATOLOGIE, PNEUMOLOGIE, ONCOLOGIE, GASTROENTEROLOGIE, KINETOTERAPIE
Fiecare sectie are urmatoarea structura:


USER 1 (INFIRMIER)

- poate updata cand a fost dezinfectat salonul, cand s-au schimbat asternuturile, daca pacientul a luat micul dejun/ pranzul/ cina.
- poate vizualiza taskurile asignate si le poate bifa ca indeplinite dupa finalizare
- nu poate vizualiza alte informatii referitoare la pacient
- poate adauga produse necesare de adus pacientului



USER 2  (ASISTENT)

- poate inregistra functiile vitale si rezultatele analizelor recente
- poate vizualiza taskurile asignate si le poate bifa ca indeplinite dupa finalizare
- poate adauga task uri pentru userul 1
- poate vizualiza toate activitatile userului 1



USER 3 (MEDIC CURANT)

- poate adauga/modifica tratament
- poate vizualiza taskurile asignate si le poate bifa ca indeplinite dupa finalizare
- poate adauga task uri pentru userul 2
- poate vizualiza activitatea userilor 1, 2 si 4



USER 4 (MEDIC DE GARDA)

- poate adauga/modifica tratament
- poate vizualiza taskurile asignate si le poate bifa ca indeplinite dupa finalizare
- poate adauga task uri pentru userul 2
- poate vizualiza activitatea userilor 1, 2 si 3



USER 5 ( SEF DE SECTIE )

- poate adauga/modifica tratament
- poate vizualiza taskurile asignate si le poate bifa ca indeplinite dupa finalizare
- poate adauga task uri pentru userul 3 si 4
- poate vizualiza activitatea userilor 1, 2, 3 si 4
- poate descarca un raport complet al pacientului pe orice perioada
- poate vizualiza si edita rapoarte financiare al sectiei, orare de lucru si contracte de munca ale sectiei respective
- poate vizualiza toti pacientii activi(internati) si inactivi(externati) din intreaga sectie, pe orice perioada



USER 6 ( MANAGER SPITAL )

- poate adauga task uri pentru userii 1, 2, 3, 4 si 5
- poate vizualiza activitatea userilor 1, 2, 3, 4 si 5
- poate vizualiza, descarca si edita TOATE rapoartele financiare, orare de lucru si contracte de munca pe intreaga unitate medicala
- poate adauga/modifica tratament si instructiuni pentru userii ASISTENT/a,  INFIRMIER/A, MEDIC CURANT, MEDIC DE GARDA, SEF DE SECTIE
- poate vizualiza activitatea userilor INFIRMIERA / ASISTENTA/ MEDIC CURANT, MEDIC DE GARDA, SEF DE SECTIE
- poate descarca raportul complet al pacientului/ pacientilor pe o perioada anume.
- poate accesa si edita TOATE rapoartele, asigurari medicale, orar de lucru si contracte de munca interne, contracte furnizori, in intreaga unitate medicala.
